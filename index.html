ABOUT US

Incorporated in 2007, Medilogix is into making of medical equipment pertaining to anesthesiology and critical care. Our technical expertise is reconciled with precision to offer you products that meets your needs perfectly. We make sure that our products are user friendly and intuitive to save time and improve efficiency.

Medilogix currently offers Infusion / Syringe Pumps and Peripheral Nerve Stimulators. More are to come and we are quite excited to notify you.

OEM Solutions: Medilogix offers OEM Solutions to entrepreneurs and established business firms/companies to further strengthen their hold in their market arena by adding products of their preference

OEM Solutions on our existing and future products and design/manufacturing of products of specific interests in medical electronics can be offered in custom application designs with lower costs, strong supply chain support and rapid deployment.
